Pumpkin Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup all-purpose
  • 2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cups of condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup of evaporated milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Prep Time: 15 minute     Cook Time 20: minutes     Yeild: 24 cupcakes


1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 12 muffin cups or line with paper liners.


2. Stir flour, pumpkin pie spice, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl. Mix pumpkin puree, evaporated milk, sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract together in a separate bowl until smooth. Add flour mixture to pumpkin mixture and stir until batter is well mixed. Fill muffin cups with batter, about 1/3 cup per cupcake.


3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, about 20 minutes. Cool cupcakes in the pan for 20 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
